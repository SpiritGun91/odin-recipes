Spanakopita

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spinach, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh dill
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 package phyllo dough
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook onion and garlic in olive oil over medium heat until softened.
  3. Add spinach and cook until wilted. Remove from heat and let cool.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine spinach mixture, feta cheese, ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, dill, and parsley. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Brush a baking dish with olive oil. Layer phyllo dough sheets, brushing each sheet with olive oil.
  6. Spread spinach mixture over phyllo dough. Top with more phyllo dough sheets, brushing each sheet with olive oil.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until golden brown.
  8. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
