Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add tomato sauce and crushed tomatoes to the skillet. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, 1 1/2 cups mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Layer with noodles, cheese mixture, and meat sauce. Repeat layers, ending with meat sauce.
  7. Sprinkle remaining mozzarella cheese on top.
  8. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  9. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
